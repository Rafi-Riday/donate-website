Our Very Recent Project

The power of the youth is immense. During various natural disasters, many youths show great courage and take a stand beside the helpless stand beside the helpless people by organaizing many NGO . Recently, our organization have received praise by providing relief for the flood victims in Sylhet-Sunamganj.

We are currently working in all flood affected areas of the country including Kurigram, Netrokona, and Sunamganj. Every two days our supply stock is transported from the Dhaka to these areas via pick-up vans.

Our Team are expressing that the general people willing to help, could buy stock, dry food or donate money to be an integral part of the benevolent initiative.
